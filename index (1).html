Who I Am

Where mountains
meet meaning

I'm Prachi — an outdoor facilitator, trek leader, and community builder from Belgaum, Karnataka. With an MBA background and a deep love for wild places, I blend leadership, human connection, and adventure to create experiences that genuinely change people.

From guiding children on high-altitude Himalayan treks to managing groups of 80 through monsoon terrain in the Sahyadris, I believe the outdoors is the greatest classroom. I collaborate with adventure brands and create content that brings the mountains to screens.

"Every trail, every tent pitched, and every challenge faced reshaped my perspective on leadership, presence, and growth."

Community

Trekkers of Belgaum

Founded in January 2024, Trekkers of Belgaum is a community built to make the outdoors accessible to everyone — across ages, fitness levels, and comfort zones. We organise treks, hikes, camping weekends, and high-altitude expeditions that bring people together through shared challenge and natural beauty.

From monsoon scrambles in the Sahyadris to spiritual journeys to Kedarnath, every outing is designed to be more than a hike — it's a turning point.
